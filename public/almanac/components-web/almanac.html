The lay of the page

— GLYPH

The orbital glyph: a circle, an off-axis ellipse, one accent dot. Reserve 38 units of layout if you want the iOS-faithful bleed.

— TYPE

Serif headline at 26 px

Body sets in Source Serif 4 at fourteen pixels with a generous line-height of one-and-a-half. Measure caps at sixty-five characters so the eye does not lose its place at the end of a line. Italic and roman alternate for emphasis without changing weight.

— and italic at thirteen pixels for asides.
